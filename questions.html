Study Habits

Main Things

Some tips for study habits are:
1. Create a dedicated study place so you won't have distractions that could interfere with your business.
2.Study in short periods of time, instead of long periods to avoid getting tired.
3.Break up big projects or things into smaller tasks that can be acomplished easier.
4.[Optional] Use techniques like the Pomodoro technique, Flowtime, Active Recall, and more.
